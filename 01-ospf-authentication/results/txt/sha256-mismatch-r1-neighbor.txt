## Phase 3: HMAC-SHA-256 authentication (R1–R2)

**Change:** Replaced MD5 authentication with a key chain using
`cryptographic-algorithm hmac-sha-256`, applied via
`ip ospf authentication key-chain OSPF-SHA` on the interface,
rather than the interface-local `message-digest-key` MD5 used.

**Mismatch behavior:** Configuring SHA-256 on R1 alone (R2 still on MD5)
caused the adjacency to drop entirely — neighbor disappeared from the
table rather than degrading to a lesser state. Confirms OSPF treats
mismatched authentication schemes as a hard failure, not just a
mismatched key value.
