## Phase 3: HMAC-SHA-256 authentication (R1–R2)

**Recovery:** Applying an identical key chain (name, key ID, key-string,
algorithm) on R2 restored `Full/-` within 15s of reconvergence.

**Key difference from MD5:** SHA-256 uses a named `key chain` object,
separate from the interface config, referenced by the interface —
rather than MD5's key sitting directly under the interface with
`message-digest-key`.
