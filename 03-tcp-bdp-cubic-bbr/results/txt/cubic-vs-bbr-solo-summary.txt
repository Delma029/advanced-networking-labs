Solo 60s runs, same link (tuned buffers, tbf burst 128Kb, ~200ms RTT):

CUBIC: 335 Mbit/s, 101 retransmits
BBR:   305 Mbit/s, 44 retransmits (56% fewer than CUBIC)

Throughput within ~10% of each other, but BBR achieved it with under
half CUBIC's retransmissions -- consistent with BBR's model-based
pacing avoiding the loss-triggered backoff CUBIC relies on to probe
the link. Note CUBIC's solo throughput here (335) is lower than earlier
solo CUBIC tests on this link (522-588 Mbit/s) -- run-to-run variance
on this link is real and non-trivial, which is precisely why the
concurrent test matters more than comparing solo numbers directly.
